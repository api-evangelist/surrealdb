SurrealDB launched Scale, a new production-focused tier of its cloud database service featuring highly-available, fault-tolerant clusters and multi-zone deployment. The offering is built on SurrealDS, a distributed storage engine designed for enterprise workloads with horizontal scalability and improved fault tolerance.
