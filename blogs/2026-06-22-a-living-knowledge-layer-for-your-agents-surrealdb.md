CocoIndex now provides a native SurrealDB connector, enabling a single declarative pipeline to manage documents, knowledge graphs, and vector indexes within one database instance. The integration addresses the challenge of maintaining fresh, contextual data for AI agents by eliminating the need for separate vector, graph, and document stores while keeping all components incrementally synchronized.
