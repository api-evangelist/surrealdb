Surrealist 3.9 introduces a complete design overhaul, a new datasets browser and data manager, an improved record inspector, and more. Updates include a redesigned sidebar, topbar, and light theme, a unified namespace and database selection menu, and an enhanced record inspector with References and Live tabs for exploring relationships and monitoring real-time changes.
